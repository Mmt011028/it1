Market research:
Market research refers to using scientific methods to collect, record, sort out and analyze the market situation purposefully and systematically, understand the current situation and development trend of the market, and provide objective and correct basis for enterprise decision-makers to make policies, market forecasts, business decisions and plans.

The role of Market Researchï¼š
1. It will help to better absorb the advanced experience and the latest technology at home and abroad, improve the production technology and management level of the enterprise
2. Provide decision-making basis for enterprise management departments and relevant principals
3. Enhance the competitiveness and viability of enterprises

Benefits of Market Research:
Through market research, we can get data and information that can help us timely understand the market economic dynamics and scientific and technological information, and provide enterprises with the latest market intelligence and technical production intelligence, so as to better learn and absorb the advanced experience and latest technology of the same industry, improve the production technology of enterprises, improve the technical level of personnel and improve the management level of enterprises, so as to improve the quality of products and accelerate the renewal of products, Enhance the competitiveness of products and enterprises and ensure the survival and development of enterprises. Marketing promotion needs to understand the communication channels and mechanisms of various information in order to find appropriate publicity and promotion carriers and methods and detailed marketing plans, which also needs to be solved by market research. Especially in the rapidly changing environment, past experience can only reduce the chance of making mistakes, but also need real-time information update to ensure the publicity and promotion in place. Generally, the market information support of powerful institutions needs to be cited in the marketing promotion, such as providing the advantage information of the enterprise in terms of consumer recognition, brand awareness, satisfaction, market share and so on, so as to meet further needs.

Lack of Market Research:
First, the samples on the network are difficult to be truly representative. The Internet is an extremely open space where anyone can participate. In addition, most Internet users belong to young, high-income, urbanized and highly educated groups. Therefore, online survey is limited by the characteristics of online audience, and the group it represents may be limited.
Second, the survey results are subject to the survey object. On the Internet, the respondents participated in the survey on the principle of complete voluntariness. Whether the questionnaire of online survey can be retrieved depends on the respondents' interest in the survey project; The questionnaire may also be repeated. This will affect the reliability of the survey results and the accuracy of the samples to a certain extent.
